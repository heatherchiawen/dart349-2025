Analogue

Design

Utilizing icons from its branding, initial statement, and calls for user-based action, connects to the overall playful theme of the website. Through these icons and other motion-based graphics, there is consistent visual activeness.

Usability

Although these interactive elements may create a sense of disarray, key information is made obvious through clear headings, menu, and recognizable buttons.

Content

The in-site games provide an interactive feature beyond the content while highlighting relevant themes amongst the brand's website and expertise.

Creativity

The icon-based nature of the site complements the straightforwardness of the text. This emphasizes the site's purpose while engaging the user in fun interactive and visual elements.
