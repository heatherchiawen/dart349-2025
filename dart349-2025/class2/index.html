BCFM

Design

The website consistently references retro technology on its loading screen, main page, and sub-pages, planting subtle clues regarding its purpose. The site only expands on these clues when the user hovers or clicks with their cursor.

Usability

While the main menu box is condensed at first glance, its sub-headings seem less clear than the numbered statistics it aims to emphasize. Hovering on these sub-headings shows numbered pages and links, however, seemingly unintuitively ordered.

Content

Considering that the website seems intended for the company's Black Friday and Cyber Monday analytics, its visual approach offers a fun way to examine data.

Creativity

Despite the lack of intuition on the user end, the site's approach to organizing information in a photo-realistic setting poses an interesting way to view information in more analog scenarios.
